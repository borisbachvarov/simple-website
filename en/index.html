About our cause

This little house was created by students with the mission to support socially vulnerable people who have limited access to essential goods. We aim to build a community rooted in mutual aid and solidarity, where people can support each other by leaving items they have in surplus and taking what they need.

Support the cause – If you have, give. If you need, take.

Goals

1. Bring

Leave everything you can bring, food, spare clothes maybe toys.

2. Share

Share with you friends, familly, neighobours or some of those distant relatives that we all 'love'.

3. Take

If you need anything, take it without hesitation.
